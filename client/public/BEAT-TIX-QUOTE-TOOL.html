⚠️ "What if I just pay the fine?" — Tell them this:

Paying the fine is not the same as paying a parking ticket. In Maryland, paying your citation = pleading GUILTY. That means:

  • Points go on your license immediately
  • Your insurance company is notified and will raise your rates — often within 30–60 days
  • The conviction stays on your MVA record for 3 years
  • If you already have points, this could push you into suspension territory
  • For incarcerable offenses — you still face potential jail time even if you pay

🚛 CDL HOLDER — CRITICAL WARNING

  • CDL drivers are held to a HIGHER standard. Convictions that carry 2 points for regular drivers can trigger CDL disqualification.
  • A serious traffic violation conviction = 60-day CDL disqualification (first offense)
  • Two serious violations in 3 years = 120-day CDL disqualification
  • DUI/DWI = 1-year CDL disqualification (minimum) — second offense = lifetime ban
  • This client could lose their job and livelihood, not just their license
  • Adjust your fee upward — the stakes are much higher. Recommend $1,500+ for most CDL cases.
